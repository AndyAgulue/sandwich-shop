Voyager Sandwiches & Seafood, a family owned lunchtime spot in Eastsound Square, offers our version of original and classic sandwiches. Our house-made sauces, pickles, ferments, and marinades set us a part from your average sandwich shop. Beyond sandwiches we serve local shellfish, small plates, salads, natural wine and craft beer. We source locally including bread from small San Juan County bakeries. Join us for lunch or call in a take out order to bring on an adventure.
